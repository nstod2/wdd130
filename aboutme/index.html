Nance

My name is Nancy Stoddart. I live in Colorado Springs, Colorado. I am married with five children ranging from sixteen to eight years old. I am currently working towards a BS in Software Development and hoping to graduate in the next two to three years. I feel very blessed to live in a time when online learning is an option. The pathway program has provided me with the ability to go back to school when I could not have attended otherwise.

I love reading, dancing, singing, crafts, playing the piano, cooking, hiking and visiting new places. I love my family and watching them grow and develop. I am learning new things everyday as a mother. I can be found at any of my children's performances or tucked away in a corner in blissful silence with a good book or movie and chocolate.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ since I was eight. I am grateful for the peace and joy that the love of my Savior has brought into my life. I don't have a favorite quote by our church leaders, but I love many quotes by church leaders. One that I like is by our prophet, President Russell M Nelson. He says, "When we love God with all our hearts, He turns our hearts to the well-being of others." I am so excited to be learning with all of you!
