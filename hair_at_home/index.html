Our Passion

Hair at Home brings professional hair care to the comfort of your own home. We will ensure a simple, convenient experience with little to no effort on your part, as we bring everything to you. Whether you need a style, cut, color, makeup or extensions, we will accommodate you in your home. Call us for your special occasions or for some pampering. We do accommodate busy schedules or even multiple individuals within a household. We simply want to make our passion, your hair style, easier on you!

Calm and Serenity

One of our goals is to ensure a luxurious and inviting atmosphere within the walls of your home during your experience with us. We hope to bring calm and serenity as we bring peace to your life. We want you to feel pampered as we give you the gift of a professional and highly individualized look.

Personal Touch

Our stylist strives to build personalized relationships with each individual. She wants to understand your style and preferences. This helps her to find a perfect balance between ease of care and individual preferences and style. Our goal is to create a unique and personalized experience for you. We want you to love your individualized and professional look!
